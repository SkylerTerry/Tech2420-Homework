Tributes: Extreme Honeymoons

Brian and Kitty Jones

Brian and Kitty wanted a memorable honeymoon and chose a cougar hunt right after their wedding. Extreme Honeymoons handled every detail, including providing archery instruction before the trip. Their weekend was tiring, exciting, and beautiful, and they even saw three cougars during the hunt. They sent snapshots of their adventure to commemorate their incredible experience.

David and Mary Farnsworth

David always dreamed of heliskiing, and their honeymoon was the perfect opportunity. With expert pilots and guides, they made six powder-filled runs a day on Kings Peak, Utah's tallest mountain. Their gear was inspected by their guide to ensure safety, and the couple praised the attention to detail that allowed them to focus on enjoying the adventure together.

Peter and Polly Renquist

Peter and Polly chose a mountain biking adventure through Moab's red rock. They stayed at a charming bed and breakfast, were provided transportation to the trailhead, and given maps and GPS gear. The weather was hot but manageable, and they look back fondly on the experience as one of the best times of their lives.
